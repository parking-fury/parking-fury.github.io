Parking Fury was created by Ukrainian game developer Andriy Pidvirnyy. The game was released in 2016 and has been a fan favorite on Coolmath Games since then, thanks to its combination of skill, strategy, and spatial awareness.

Parking Fury only has 10 levels, but each one grows in difficulty. The fastest recorded time on Parking Fury is around 7-and-a-half minutes, achieved by an experienced player. Beginners can expect Parking Fury to take around 20 to 30 minutes on the first attempt.
